# Code of Conduct

Meeting Mojiokoshi is a small project for local meeting recording and
transcription. Keep discussion practical, respectful, and focused on improving
the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when reporting bugs, requesting changes, or reviewing code.
- Assume good faith, but be specific about technical problems.
- Keep legal, privacy, and recording-consent concerns visible when they affect
  users.
- Avoid sharing private meeting audio, transcripts, credentials, or personal
  data in issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private or sensitive data without clear permission.
- Posting vulnerability details publicly before maintainers can assess them.
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Reporting

For security issues, follow `SECURITY.md`.

For conduct concerns, open a minimal GitHub issue asking for a private contact
channel. Do not include sensitive details in the public issue.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or delete content, and may block users when
needed to keep the project safe and usable.
